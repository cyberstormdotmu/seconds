Pricing

What is a Silverwing price curve?

The Silverwing Price Curve is our way of displaying the price of a product.

When you're browsing products on our site, you'll notice each one has a graph alongside it. This graph is known as the Price Curve' and it allows you to see you how many units of a product have already been sold and what the current price of that product is (i.e. what you'd pay today, excluding VAT). You'll also be able to see what the price of that product could be in the future, if further sales are made. We update our Price Curve pricing information in real-time!

You'll also see that each Price Curve has an Offer Period End Date displayed next to it. Each product will only be available for sale up to the Offer Period End Date shown. Once the Offer Period ends, no further sales can be accepted for the product under that specific Price Curve. That doesn't necessarily mean we won't re-stock that particular product, it just means it's no longer available to buy under the terms of the previous Price Curve.

Because we update our Price Curve information in real-time, you'll be able to see if the Current Price drops. If the Current Price of a product drops to less than what you paid for it, we'll credit you the difference in real-time! You can find out more about Silverwing Credits under "What are Silverwing Credits?". 

What price do I pay?

You'll pay the Current Price of a product at the time of your purchase - this will be clearly shown on the Price Curve which is next to the product itself. The Current Price excludes VAT.

You can find out more about our Price Curves under "What is a Silverwing Price Curve?"

You can find out more about VAT under "What about VAT?"

What are Silverwing Credits?

At Silverwing we reward you for smarter buying together!

In a nutshell, Silverwing Credits are the difference between the price you paid for a product and the current price of that product (shown on its Price Curve)! So if you paid £100 for a product which we're now selling for £80, we'll credit you the £20 difference instantly! Simple!

Silverwing Credits (£) = Price you Paid (£) - Current Price (£)


You can spend your Silverwing Credits on any Silverwing product or have them sent to your bank account as cash.

Your Silverwing Credit will either show as available or pending. Available credits are ready to be spent now! Pending credits will be all yours to spend once your products have been paid for in full! When that happens, your pending credits will become available.

You'll continue to receive Silverwing Credits until the offer period of the product you bought ends. See "What is a Silverwing Price Curve?" for more information on Offer Period End Dates.

Also see Why are my Silverwing Credits pending?

You should refer to your Silverwing account holder agreement for details of any applicable Terms and Conditions in relation to Silverwing Credits

What can I do with Silverwing Credits?

You can spend your Silverwing Credits on any Silverwing product or have them sent to your bank account as cash.

Spend Silverwing Credits on Silverwing

You can spend your available Silverwing Credits on any Silverwing order - just look for the pay by Silverwing Credit' option at check out! You can pay for all, or just part of your order, using your available Credits.

Withdraw Silverwing Credits as Cash

You can request for your available Silverwing Credits to be sent to your registered bank account within your account page. We aim to process any withdrawal requests as soon as possible, but in any event it shouldn't take longer than 10 working days for your Silverwing Credits to reach your bank account.

Our Withdrawal Policy:

  • We can only pay Silverwing Credits to the bank account registered on your Silverwing account;
  • You can make up to two withdrawal requests per day;
  • We will send payments via BACS;
  • You will receive a credit note to document your withdrawal;
  • You can withdraw an available Silverwing Credit balance of £1.00 and over. Amounts below £1.00 cannot be processed; and
  • If your credits are shown as pending, you will not be able to withdraw them until they are shown as available (see "What are Silvering Credits" for further information in relation to available and pending credits).

Why are my Silverwing Credits pending?

Silverwing Credits are shown as pending typically if you've made your purchase using a Supplier Credit Facility and therefore the supplier hasn't yet received full payment for your product or if you have made your purchase via a card payment and we are waiting to receive funds into our account.

You'll be able to use your pending Silverwing Credits once your products have been paid for in full! When that happens, your pending credits will become available.

Returns and VAT

Who do I contact about returns?

If you wish to return any products bought on Silverwing, you will need to contact the supplier who fulfilled your order. The supplier's contact information is on the order confirmation email we sent to you after your made your purchase.The supplier will then process any return requests in line with their own returns policy.

Please bear in mind that your right to return a product may be affected if you have already withdrawn or spent Silverwing Credits originating from the purchase of the product you wish to return.

What about VAT?

The price you pay for a product (the Current Price) is always exclusive of VAT. VAT at the usual rate will be added and shown during the checkout process.

When you buy products through Silverwing, the supplier who's fulfilling your order will send you an invoice which includes a breakdown of your VAT calculation.

What about VAT on Silverwing Credits?

If you earn any Silverwing Credits from an order, you will receive a credit note from the supplier which will include a breakdown of any VAT you're being refunded. It is your company's responsibility account for any VAT refunds.

You can normally expect to receive a credit note in relation to your Silverwing Credits at the earliest of any of the following stages:
  1. If you spend or withdraw any available Silverwing Credits earnt by buying the product, before stages 2 or 3 below occur
  2. If the Minimum Price* of the product is reached as per its price curve
  3. If it's the end of the product's Offer Period (and therefore no further Silverwing Credits are owing).

* The Minimum Price of a product is the lowest possible price a product will be sold for under its Silverwing Price Curve.
