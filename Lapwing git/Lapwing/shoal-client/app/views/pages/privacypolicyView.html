Cookies

We use “cookies” on the Platform.  Cookies are small data files stored on your device when using websites and apps.  Cookies help us monitor and improve the functionality and usage of our Platform and your experience of the Platform.

We use cookies to see which areas and features are popular and to count visits to our Platform, to recognise you as a returning visitor and to tailor your experience of the Platform according to your preferences. 

You can delete or block cookies on the Platform through your browser settings at any time. For more general information about cookies including the difference between session and persistent cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
